Membership

Membership at St Timothy Tennis Club is very affordable. The 2019 season Adult membership is $335 per year. Family membership is $375. We also offer a Junior membership for $25. New members initiation fee of $25 is waived.

Members can bring guests for up to five visits and the member is responsible for paying the $5 per visit guest fee. We also ask that members complete a guest card so we can follow up with our guests.
