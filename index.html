WELCOME to our Club

St Timothy has been a tennis club in Catonsville, MD for more than 60 years.

Our season generally runs from late April to October of each year. Playing hours are from 9 am to dusk, 7 days a week.

We have about 100 members and we welcome new members. There are 5 har-tru clay courts. This surface is softer than hard courts and easier on the joints.

Why Choose Us

We provide a friendly atmosphere and are conventiently located near midtown Catonsville, MD.
